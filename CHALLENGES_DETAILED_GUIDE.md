## ❓ FAQs

**Q: Can I complete the same challenge multiple times per day?**  
A: No, each challenge can only be completed once per day. They reset at midnight.

**Q: What happens if I miss a day?**  
A: Nothing! Just continue the next day. Your points and level remain.

**Q: Can I do challenges in any order?**  
A: Yes! Complete them in any order that works for you.

**Q: Do I need to complete all challenges?**  
A: No, complete as many as you want. Even 1-2 per day is great!

**Q: How do I know if a challenge is completed?**  
A: It will show "Completed Today" badge and won't be clickable until tomorrow.

**Q: Can I undo a completed challenge?**  
A: No, once marked complete, it cannot be undone for that day.
