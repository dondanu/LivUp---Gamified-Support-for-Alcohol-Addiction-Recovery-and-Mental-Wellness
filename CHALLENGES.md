# MindFusion Daily Challenges

This document lists all available daily challenges in the MindFusion recovery app. Complete these challenges to earn points, level up, and support your recovery journey.

## Challenge Categories

- **Wellness**: Activities focused on mental and emotional well-being
- **Health**: Physical health and fitness activities
- **Reflection**: Journaling and self-reflection exercises
- **Education**: Learning and personal development
- **Social**: Connection and community engagement

---

## Easy Challenges (10-15 Points)

### Wellness
1. **Morning Meditation** - 15 points
   - Start your day with 5 minutes of meditation

2. **Art/Creative Activity** - 15 points
   - Engage in creative expression

3. **Nature Walk** - 15 points
   - Spend 20 minutes outdoors

4. **Practice Mindfulness** - 15 points
   - Do a 10-minute mindfulness exercise

5. **Music Therapy** - 10 points
   - Listen to uplifting music for 30 minutes

6. **Deep Breathing** - 10 points
   - Practice 5 minutes of deep breathing

### Health
7. **Healthy Meal** - 10 points
   - Prepare and eat a nutritious meal

8. **Stretch/Yoga** - 15 points
   - Do 15 minutes of stretching or yoga

### Reflection
9. **Journal Entry** - 10 points
   - Write in your recovery journal

10. **Gratitude List** - 10 points
    - Write down 3 things you're grateful for

11. **Write Affirmations** - 10 points
    - Write 5 positive affirmations

### Education
12. **Read Recovery Material** - 15 points
    - Read for 15 minutes about recovery

13. **Learn Something New** - 10 points
    - Watch a tutorial or read an article

14. **Listen to Podcast** - 15 points
    - Listen to a recovery or wellness podcast

### Social
15. **Social Connection** - 20 points
    - Have a meaningful conversation with a friend

## Tips for Success

1. **Start Small**: Begin with easy challenges to build momentum
2. **Be Consistent**: Try to complete at least one challenge daily
3. **Mix Categories**: Balance different types of activities for holistic recovery
4. **Track Progress**: Use the app to monitor your streaks and achievements
5. **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge every completed challenge, no matter how small

## Points & Leveling System

Points earned from challenges contribute to:
- Your total points score
- Level progression
- Achievement unlocks
- Avatar upgrades
- Streak bonuses

Keep completing challenges to unlock new levels and achievements!

## Need Help?

If you're struggling with any challenge:
- Reach out to your support network
- Use the SOS feature in the app
- Modify challenges to fit your current abilities
- Remember: Progress, not perfection!
